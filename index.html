Text to teapot
Text to teapot is a web journal about my personal experience in experimenting with GPT-3 and 3D modelling and the relationship I developed with it.

The thesis project aimed to test the current capabilities of Ai, its limitations and its implementation or replacement to the designer's work. While remaining an intimate production, the journal's hyperlinks and bibliography allow the reader to explore the technical stages of my research.

l'éducation Sexuelle
L'Éducation Sexuelle is a series of vector illustrations created to accompany Abram Tomasi's story of the same title. These illustrations metaphorically explore the journey of sexual discovery, depicting it through evocative images of bodies interwoven with the organic growth of mushrooms.

The style aims to capture the essence of this profound human experience tracing its shapes and awkwardness.
Orlando
Inspired by Virginia Woolf's novel, "Orlando", these three short stories explore the growth of identity in literary characters.

I chose to delve into this concept through a personal case study, following Orlando, an active reader. Orlando ventures through various situations, at times living others' stories to discover his own identity. This journey takes him through three unique environments, where he grapples with community, interactions with others, and self-discovery.

Orlando is hosted entirely on a dedicated instagram account, as an experiment on solely digital fruition of illustrated publishing.

Ötzy senza cuore
In this project, I had the pleasure of illustrating a short story by Abram Tomasi, a narrative that unfolds during a night of revelry, encounters, and betrayal on New Year's Eve.

Drawing inspiration from Abram's metaphor, I portrayed him as Ötzy (a wet mummy preserved in Bozen), a character who embarks on adventures amidst a wild crowd of social animals, only to find himself in a chilling, frozen ending.
Laura
Laura is short illustrated story where I reflect about FOMO and loosing friends, leaving the university circle, even one of my best friends.

While I tell Laura about how I miss being and producing ideas with her, our characters transform in color, becoming animals we used sometimes before as masks. Longing for the cultural exchange that I have long lost, I wish to eat and digest her, but end up the way around, defecated and left behind.
Hotèl
This short fragmented story is the expression of a specific feeling. The one of dissociating while listening to the loved one, a boy with many stories and adventures I like to experience again in my memories with its strange characters and glances of intimacy.

All tales take place in a three stars hotel in Padova.
Sembravi un tossichello
One of my first self-referential stories. Through the conversations we exchanged I quickly traverse the evolution of a past relationship of mine, from meeting in the midst of macaques to our farewell one night and my departure for Stockholm.

The narrative captures a candid look to the swift progression of closeness and separation.
